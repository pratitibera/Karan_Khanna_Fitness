FAQS
What can I expect from online personal training?
Online personal training allows you to have consultations from the comfort of your home. Karan shall monitor your progress and technique through live classes* and provide 24/7 online support through WhatsApp.
Note: the number of live classes depends on what plan you opt for.
Do you offer customised programs?
Yes, all our programs are tailored for each individual keeping in mind their personal history, preferences, goals, and lifestyle.
Are your programmes suitable for beginners?
Absolutely! We believe that it is never too early or too late to start your journey towards a healthier life. The difficulty level of the programs is completely personalised, so beginners will not find it difficult to start exercising.
Do you offer nutrition plans for vegans?
Inclusivity lies at the core of Karan Khanna Fitness. As all our programs are tailored to suit every individual, we offer nutrition plans for all types of diets including fully vegan diets.
How long does it take to see results?
Visual results are highly subjective as they depend on several factors such as your baseline fitness, your goals, and how strictly you adhere to your plans.
Rather than fixating on visual results, we encourage clients to enjoy the mindset changes that are accompanied by the lifestyle changes brought about by the programs.
How can I communicate with my trainer?
You may reach out to Karan through WhatsApp or email. All of our programs also include a certain number of live sessions during which you may discuss your needs with Karan.
What type of equipment do I need to get started?
We offer both home-based and gym-based programs as part of our fitness consultancy. Any equipment you may need shall depend on your plans and individual preferences. The only thing you truly need to get started is the will to become healthier.
Do I need to be in good shape already to start strengthtraining?
Not at all. Broadly speaking, strength training is suitable for almost everyone. For beginners, the fitness plans will work on gradually building your strength and increasing difficulty levels at a sustainable pace.
How can I get started with my consultation?
To get started on your journey, simply reach out to us by clicking on Consultation at the top of this page and filling out the details or reach out to us through the Contact page.
How do the payment procedure and refund policy work?
Once a consultation is already underway, we have a no-cancellation policy. Further refund requests under special circumstances are entirely under Karan Khanna’s discretion.
