ABOUT ME
MEET KARAN KHANNA
Exercise and Nutrition Specialist and Wellness Coach, Karan Khanna believes in the transformative power of adopting discipline and consistency as a way of life.
VISION
Making progress in health is more than just numbers on a scale or measurements. Sustainable growth includes mental and behavioral changes that can only result from a sustainable mindset. Karan Khanna’s vision is to help you use physical fitness as a stepping stone to overall well-being.
GOAL
Karan Khanna will help you create new, healthy habits with exercise and nutrition programs that are created exclusively for you at a pace that is sustainable for you. His goal is to empower you to build a mindset that unlocks your full potential and makes fitness a part of your lifestyle.
KARAN’S JOURNEY
2019
2019 was a difficult year for me. I got out of shape and my overall physical and mental health took a turn for the worse. The whole experience was extremely challenging, but it also strengthened my resolve. I knew that I had to step up and build something of my own.
2020
The lockdowns in 2020 were a nightmare for everyone, including me. I was left at an all-time low, both professionally and personally. After months of struggle, I decided to take charge of my life and pursue professional certifications in fitness and nutrition. The whole experience pushed my life in an entirely different direction. I became an intern at a reputed gym and started getting my own clients.
2021
The upward streak that began in 2020 continued in 2021 as well. I was getting more clients and finally found fulfillment in my career. More importantly, being disciplined and consistent with my workouts gave me the perspective I needed and my mental health improved immensely. Having felt the power of adopting a healthier lifestyle, I wanted to do the same for my clients and help them become the best they can be.
2022
2022 is the year where I'm pushing my limits and expanding my horizons. Grateful for the immense growth and development I have witnessed in the past year, I decided to be the best I can be and take Karan Khanna Fitness to even greater heights. As I continue to learn and grow in this ever-expanding field, I feel more confident than ever.
WHY CHOOSE
KARAN KHANNA
Being healthy doesn’t have to be complicated. At Karan Khanna Fitness, you will find scientific programs that simplify the science behind exercise and nutrition to deliver incredible results and guarantee long- term success. What makes Karan unique is his emphasis on the impact of fitness and discipline on the mental health and well-being of a person. Karan believes that fitness is an upward journey that can transform your way of life.
